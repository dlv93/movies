For a movie made in 1903, the visuals are strikingly clear for the era. It's a silent film that's does fairly well for portraying the life of Jesus Christ. For someone who doesn't know the story of Jesus' life, the scenes would probably be hard to interpret what's going on. It can come across as pretty cheesy with some of the gestures and props used on set, but all in all I think it was a fair movie that is clear about it's intent and message.
